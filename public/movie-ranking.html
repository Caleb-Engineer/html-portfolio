The Best Movies According to Caleb

My Top 3 Favorite Movies

The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

This movie is more than a tale of adventure; it's a profound exploration
of growth and transformation. It begins in the idyllic Shire,
a symbol of innocence and home, where Frodo and Sam are bound
by self-imposed limitations. As they embark on a perilous journey,
they transcend those boundaries, evolving in character and perspective.
The contrast between the peaceful Shire and the demanding road ahead
enchants viewers and draws them into a world where bravery and
friendship conquer fear. My love for this film lies not just in its epic
storytelling but in its ability to reveal the hidden strength within the most
unlikely heroes, reflecting a universal truth about human potential and resilience.

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse

I could go on about this movie all day. I love colorful, creative, and highly rich animation films.
I especially love how this film broke out of the all too predicatable Spider-Man
mold and surprised me in a way that will be cherished for years to come!

The Incredibles

Great watch! Very wholesome and creative!
